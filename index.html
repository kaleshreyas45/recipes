Veg Manchurian

In a wok/ kadhai, add oil. Add ginger, garlic and green chillies, mix well and cook for 2 minutes. Add soy sauce, mix well. Add spring onion greens and coriander leaves, mix well and cook for 2 minutes. Add cornstarch slurry and mix well. Add Manchurian balls, mix well and cook for another 2 minutes. Serve hot.

Panner Chilli Roll

In a bowl, add cornstarch, salt to taste, water as required and mix it well. Add paneer into the mixture and marinate well. Heat oil in a kadai, fry on medium flame until crisp and brown in color. Remove on an absorbent paper. Keep it aside for further use. In a bowl, add carrot, cabbage, salt to taste, chilli vinegar, sugar and mix everything well. Keep aside for further use. Take a small portion of the dough and roll it thin using a rolling pin. Heat flat pan and roast it on both sides, apply some oil and cook until light brown from both sides. Keep aside for further use.
